# Sample image credits

These bundled sample photos are used only as demo inputs for the classifier.

- **hopper.jpg** — Grace Hopper, U.S. Navy photograph. Public domain (work of
  the U.S. federal government). Source: Wikimedia Commons.
- **dog.jpg**, **pug.jpg** — photos from [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com), retrieved
  via [Lorem Picsum](https://picsum.photos) (image IDs 237 and 1025). Used under the
  [Unsplash License](https://unsplash.com/license), which permits free use.

Users can also upload their own images; nothing is uploaded to a server — all
inference and attacks run in the browser.
